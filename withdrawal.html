Withdrawal Instructions

Step-by-step Withdrawal Guide:
  1. Select your country from the options below.
  2. Follow the withdrawal process specific to your country.
  3. Send withdrawal request details to support.
  4. Wait for admin approval to process your withdrawal.
Available Country Options:
  • India: Bank Transfer (Provide account details to support)
  • Pakistan: Easypaisa / JazzCash withdrawal (Contact support for details)
  • UAE: Local Bank Transfer (IBAN required)
  • Other Countries: Cryptocurrency withdrawal to your wallet address

Note: Withdrawals are manually approved by admin. Please be patient.
